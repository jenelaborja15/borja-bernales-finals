About Us

The College of Computer Studies is committed to three main goals:
Sharing knowledge and expertise through teaching.
Engaging in Computer Science research.
Developing Information Technology products.
The college emphasizes actual implementation of theories,
project-oriented learning, and real-world environments.
It offers undergraduate programs in computer science,
computer engineering, and information technology.
